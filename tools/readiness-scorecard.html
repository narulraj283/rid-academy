    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the Insurance Independence Readiness Scorecard?

    The Readiness Scorecard is a 15-question assessment that evaluates your dental practice's readiness to reduce PPO insurance dependence. Based on your answers, you'll receive a score and a personalized roadmap with specific recommendations tailored to your practice's strengths and weaknesses.

    What do the different readiness levels mean?

    60–75 (Ready to Launch): Your practice has the foundation to begin reducing insurance dependence. 45–59 (Almost There): You're close—focus on your lowest-scoring areas. 30–44 (Building Foundation): You need to strengthen key fundamentals first. 15–29 (Early Stage): Start with the basics like patient acquisition and team alignment.

    How long does the assessment take?

    The Readiness Scorecard takes about 5–10 minutes to complete. You'll answer one question at a time, and the assessment automatically advances as you select your answers.

    Can I retake the assessment?

    Yes! You can retake the assessment as many times as you want. Many practices use it to track progress over time by retaking it after addressing the recommended areas.

    What if I score low? Should I drop PPO plans anyway?

    A lower score simply means there are prerequisites to address first. The assessment shows exactly which areas need attention and provides a step-by-step roadmap. Many practices in our community have successfully reduced PPO dependence by following the tier-specific recommendations.
