Frequently Asked Questions

What is a strategy session?

A strategy session is a personalized consultation where we discuss your practice goals, current challenges with insurance dependence, and explore how RID Academy can help you transition to a more profitable fee-for-service model.

Is there a cost for a strategy session?

Strategy sessions are complimentary for qualified dental practice owners. We'll discuss your situation and determine if RID Academy is the right fit for your practice.

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary by practice, but most practice owners begin implementing strategies immediately and see measurable improvements within 90 days. Long-term success requires commitment to the process.

Can solo practices benefit from RID Academy?

Absolutely! Our coaching works for solo practitioners and multi-doctor practices. We customize our approach to your specific practice model and goals.
