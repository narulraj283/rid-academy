CORE METHODOLOGY
================
The article teaches a complete fee-setting system:

1. Calculate overhead percentage
2. Determine desired profit margin (25-35% target)
3. Map top 20 procedures
4. Research market UCR data
5. Calculate time and material costs
6. Set fee targets using formula: (Overhead + Materials) ÷ (1 - Profit Margin %)
7. Validate against market reality
