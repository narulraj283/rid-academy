========================================
KEY FORMULAS & NUMBERS:
========================================

CORE FORMULA:
(Overhead Cost + Material Cost) ÷ (1 - Desired Profit Margin %) = Target Fee

EXAMPLE CALCULATION:
Crown with $600 overhead allocation + $120 materials
Targeting 30% profit
= ($720) ÷ (1 - 0.30)
= $720 ÷ 0.70
= $1,029 minimum fee

SAMPLE PROCEDURE FEES:
- D1110 (Prophy): $130 (75th percentile)
- D0120 (X-rays): $90 (75th percentile)
- D2740 (Crown PFM): $1,200 (75th percentile)
- D2750 (Crown Ceramic): $1,100 (75th percentile)
- D6010 (Implant): $2,100 (75th percentile)

FEE INCREASE IMPACT:
5-8% increase = $50,000-$150,000 additional annual profit

TARGET BENCHMARKS:
- Overhead: 60% (typical range: 50-70%)
- Net Profit Margin: 25-35%
- Fee Positioning: 75-85th percentile of UCR
