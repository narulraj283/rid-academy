About RID Academy

RID Academy was founded with a singular mission: to help dental practice owners reduce their insurance dependence and build thriving, profitable fee-for-service practices.

The dental industry is rapidly changing. Insurance reimbursements continue to decline, administrative burdens keep increasing, and practice owners are looking for proven alternatives. RID Academy is the education arm of the insurance independence movement in dentistry, combining cutting-edge practice management strategies with digital marketing expertise.

We teach dental practice owners the exact strategies that have helped over 2,200 practices successfully transition to fee-for-service models. Our approach is based on real-world experience, not theory. Every strategy we teach has been tested, refined, and proven effective in actual dental practices.

Our Mission

"To give every dental practice owner the knowledge, tools, and confidence to practice dentistry on their own terms."
