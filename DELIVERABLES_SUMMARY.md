### Seven-Step Fee-Setting Process
1. Calculate True Overhead Rate
2. Determine Desired Net Profit Margin
3. Map Your Top 20 Procedures
4. Research Market UCR
5. Calculate Time and Resource Costs
6. Set Fee Targets
7. Validate Against Market Data

### The Core Formula
```
(Overhead Cost + Material Cost) ÷ (1 - Desired Profit Margin %) = Fee
```

## SPECIAL SECTIONS INCLUDED

### Diagnostic Checklist
7-question checklist to assess if fees are too low:
- Below 50th percentile UCR
- Net profit below 20%
- Patients regularly requesting discounts
- Clinical decisions based on patient finances
- Patient attrition with 5-10% increases
- Substantial PPO write-offs (50%+)
- Working longer hours for same revenue

### Common Mistakes (with Fixes)
1. Setting fees based only on competitor pricing
2. Frequent fee changes
3. Across-the-board percentage increases
4. Ignoring material cost inflation
5. Setting fees without understanding overhead
