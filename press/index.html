About RID Academy

RID Academy is the #1 resource for dental practice owners reducing dependence on PPO insurance plans. Since 2020, we've guided over 10,000 dental professionals through the transition from insurance-dependent practices to thriving, independent businesses. Our mission is to empower dentists to practice on their own terms with proven strategies, education, and community support.

Key Statistics:

  • 10,000+ dental professionals in our community
  • 350+ expert articles and guides on dental practice management
  • 30 comprehensive PDF guides covering all aspects of insurance independence
  • Weekly podcast with industry experts and successful practice owners
  • Free tools including PPO Write-Off Calculator and Readiness Scorecard
