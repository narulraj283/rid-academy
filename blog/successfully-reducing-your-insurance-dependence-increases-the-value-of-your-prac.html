Successfully Reducing Your Insurance Dependence INCREASES the Value of Your Prac

3 min readEpisode 266

In this episode, Gary and Narin discuss the benefits of reducing insurance dependence in your dental practice to increase its value. They emphasize that a practice’s value is closely tied to its profitability and that reducing insurance dependence can significantly enhance that profitability. They also mention the option of selling your practice to a Dental Service Organization (DSO) as an alternative pathway. Focusing on attracting fee-for-service patients and dropping unnecessary PPO plans can boost your practice’s income and long-term value. Enhancing the value of your practice is a valuable long-term strategy to consider.
