Are You A Victim of One and Done’ Thinking?

3 min readEpisode 254

In this episode, Gary discusses the “one-and-done thinking” mindset that often leads to incomplete progress. Focusing on dentistry, he highlights how this attitude can hinder growth in various aspects. Gary emphasizes four key areas where this mindset emerges: Google reviews, verbal skills training, transitioning from PPO plans, and team member recognition. He encourages continuous effort rather than assuming tasks are complete after initial attempts. Gary’s insights underline the need for sustained commitment to achieve lasting success, reminding listeners to avoid the “one and done” approach in their professional and personal endeavors.
