First Things First

3 min readEpisode 181

Author Stephen Covey was a master at explaining how we need to organize our lives around values and priorities. If we fail to do so, we can easily fall into a lifestyle full of quiet desperation. First Things First!

As a dentist, what was your aspiration? Gary bets that part of your aspiration was to truly take care of people and provide the highest quality dentistry. But are you able to truly enjoy this feeling of fulfillment? 

In this episode, Gary and Naren address how the PPO environment constrains dentists from living their dreams and aspirations and how it leads to a life of quiet desperation! Gary provides advice to dentists on how they can change this lifestyle and reach their true potential!
