What Are Umbrella PPO Plans?

Complexities in Resigning from PPOs

Gary explains that insurance companies intentionally create complex and exhausting resignation processes to discourage dentists from leaving umbrella PPO plans.

This “strategy of fatigue” relies on overwhelming staff with paperwork, multiple steps, and lack of clarity—hoping they’ll eventually give up and stay in network.

I thought I was out of it. Now I’m not out of it. Now I have to send letters out. Now I have to do this and this and this. And it’s fatigue. It’s an intentional fatigue dynamic to get you fatigued and give up. And that’s what happens with so many insurance coordinators.
