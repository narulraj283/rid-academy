Is It Possible to Negotiate Your Fee Schedule with Dental Insurance Companies?

3 min read

Many dentists wonder if they can negotiate higher reimbursement rates with insurance companies. The reality may surprise you.

The Negotiation Myth

The short answer is that traditional PPO fee schedule negotiation is extremely difficult and rarely yields meaningful results. Insurance companies set their fee schedules based on market data and have little incentive to pay individual providers more. However, there are some nuances worth understanding.

When Negotiation Might Work

In rare cases, practices in underserved areas or those with unique specializations may have some leverage. If you're the only provider within a certain radius or offer specialized services that the network lacks, the insurance company may be willing to adjust fees to keep you in-network. But these situations are uncommon.

A Better Strategy Than Negotiation

Rather than spending energy trying to negotiate marginally better PPO rates, consider investing that energy in building a practice that doesn't depend on insurance fee schedules at all. The time you spend trying to get an extra five percent from an insurance company could be spent developing systems that attract fee-for-service patients willing to pay your full fees.

The Real Path to Higher Revenue

Focus on providing exceptional patient experiences, building a strong brand in your community, and developing a membership plan for uninsured patients. These strategies consistently produce better financial outcomes than any PPO fee negotiation ever could.
