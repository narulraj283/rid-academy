Biggest mistake practice owners make when building a website

3 min readEpisode 226

It’s time for another episode of the Less insurance dependence podcast about building websites. Naren and Gary talk about the biggest mistake practice owners make when building their websites.

Naren is an expert on Digital Marketing and has been helping dentists to grow their presence online. He’s done hundreds of workshops with dentists across the USA and Canada, helping them build effective websites that are easy to navigate and search engine-friendly.

Naren and Gary talk about why having a website is so important, how you can rank on google with organic search, and what mistakes dentists make on their websites.
