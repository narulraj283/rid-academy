A New Patient Experience that You and Your Patients Will Love

3 min readEpisode 311

In this episode of the Less Insurance Dependence Podcast, co-hosts Gary Takacs and Naren Arulrajah explore the concept of creating a memorable and impactful new patient experience in dental practices. They emphasize the importance of designing an experience that aligns with patient expectations while achieving the practice’s goals for comprehensive care. The hosts discuss the significance of the first impression a dental practice makes on a new patient. A well-structured new patient experience can lead to five-star reviews, long-term patient retention, and increased case acceptance. The focus is on delivering an experience that balances the patient’s desire for a cleaning with the practice’s need for comprehensive records and a thorough examination. To help practices create this ideal experience, Gary and Naren introduce a seven-step process.
