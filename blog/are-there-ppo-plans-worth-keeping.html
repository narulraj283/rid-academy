Are There PPO Plans Worth Keeping?

3 min readEpisode 264

In this podcast episode, Naren and Gary discuss the current challenges of keeping PPO plans for dental practices. They highlight the significant increase in dental costs since COVID-19 and the strain this places on practice finances. The conversation explores the idea of treating PPO adjustments as a marketing expense and the limitations of staying in-network. They ultimately question the viability of keeping PPO plans and emphasize the need to reduce reliance on them for the success of a dental practice.
