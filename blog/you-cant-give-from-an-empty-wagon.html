You Can’t Give from an Empty Wagon

3 min readEpisode 186

Have you been planning to get involved with a charity? There are many avenues you could take to pursue this! It would be best if you choose your charity; instead of having a charity choose you.  

Did you know that being involved with PPO plans will restrict your ability and time to get involved with charities close to your heart. In this episode, Gary and Naren discuss how you can get involved with your local and national charities. He also emphasizes why you should choose a charity and not let the charity choose you.

Gary discusses how being in a PPO plan will limit your bandwidth to contribute to charities. Gary and Naren provide details on charity organizations and how you can involve your whole team in this process. Listen to the episode to learn more!
