Can I Raise My Fees on PPO Plans?

19 min readEpisode 178

One of the hide-and-seek games Insurance companies play with the dentists is that they don’t tell the dentists the maximum fees they can schedule. Instead, they hope that the fees schedule you submit is lower than their allowed fees! So how would you know if your fees are set below the maximum allowed level?

In this episode, Gary and Naren discuss the answer to the question Can I Raise My Fees on PPO Plans? and the resources which can help you with this!
