The PPO Umbrella Nightmare

3 min readEpisode 284

Are you tired of PPO plans dictating your dentistry and income? This episode exposes the dark side of PPO umbrella plans and how they can sneakily increase your write-offs, costing you thousands of dollars. Tune in and learn how to successfully untangle yourself from this web of PPO plans that keep you trapped in a system that doesn’t value your practice.

Gary shares some real-world examples of dentists who have successfully broken free from PPO dependence and grow their practices and how they strategically did this. Furthermore, learn the 3 most essential steps to be prepared to resign from PPO plans successfully.

If you are a dentist who thinks you can not get out of this complicated web, this episode will help you see how you can do it! Tune in to the Less Insurance Dependence podcast and escape the PPO Umbrella Nightmare!
