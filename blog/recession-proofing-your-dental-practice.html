Recession Proofing Your Dental Practice

3 min readEpisode 321

This episode of The Less Insurance Dependence Podcast focuses on “Recession Proofing Your Dental Practice.” Gary Takacs and Naren Arulrajah share straightforward tips to help dental practices thrive during challenging economic times. They emphasize building patient trust, creating a relationship-driven environment, and providing unique reasons for patients to choose your practice, such as state-of-the-art technology and a focus on prevention. The episode also highlights the pitfalls of relying heavily on dental insurance networks and encourages practices to attract patients who value quality care.

The simple answer to recession-proofing your practice is to give patients lots of reasons to come to you.

Recession-proofing your dental practice is about creating a mindset to thrive even in the worst case because if you can do well in the worst times, you’ll crush it when things are good.
