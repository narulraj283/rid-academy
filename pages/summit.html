Is the summit free to attend? +

Yes! The summit is completely free for all dental professionals. No hidden fees, no credit card required. Just register and join us.

Absolutely. The 2026 summit is accredited for 5+ hours of continuing education credits. You'll receive your CE certificate after the event concludes.

The summit runs for 3 days in November, with sessions running 3–4 hours per day. You can attend live or watch on-demand if you register as a community member.
