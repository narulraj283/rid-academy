The Broken System

Why We Exist

The Problem is Clear

Over 95% of dental practices are locked into PPO contracts that haven't kept pace with inflation, cost of living, or the true cost of delivering care. The result is staggering:

  • 42-45% Average revenue lost to PPO write-offs annually
  • $250K+ Typical annual loss for a single-doctor practice
  • Growing 31.8% of US dentists are actively planning to drop PPOs

But the path forward is unclear. Dentists lack the tools, guidance, and community to make the transition confidently. RID Academy exists to change that.
